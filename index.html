FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know before working with us.

How long does it take to build a website?
Most simple business websites and landing pages are delivered within 3–5 days. Larger projects with custom features (booking systems, databases, etc.) typically take 7–14 days. We provide a clear timeline before starting any project.
How much does a website cost?
Our pricing starts from ₹999 for simple landing pages and goes up based on features, number of pages, and complexity. Contact us with your requirements and we'll give you a detailed, no-obligation quote.
Can I get both a website and graphic designs?
Absolutely! We offer combo packages where you get your website built plus a full set of social media graphics, posters, and marketing materials — all at a bundled discount.
Will my website work on mobile phones?
Yes, always! Every website we build is 100% mobile responsive — it will look and work perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. We test on multiple devices before delivery.
Do you offer revisions?
Yes! We offer up to 3 free revisions on every project. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we won't consider a project complete until you're happy with the result.
How do I get started?
Simply fill out the contact form below or message us directly on WhatsApp. We'll get back within a few hours to discuss your requirements, timeline, and pricing. It's that easy!
